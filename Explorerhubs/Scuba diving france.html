Scuba diving

Scuba diving is an underwater swimming activity involving the use of self-contained underwater breathing apparatus (SCUBA). Scuba diving is mainly done for the attraction of the unattainable undersea world. It is one area of nature that humankind has not been able to control fully; we are not able to breathe underwater. Hence, scuba diving allows us to be in that underwater world, even if it is just for a limited amount of time.

Why Do People Choose Scuba Diving?

Though not being able to breath underwater requires you to come back to the surface every time to gasp for air. Le Tombant Des Americains

The city of Nice is known for its many amazing diving spots, and one of the best spots for scuba diving in Nice, France is Le Tombant Des Americains, a diving site known for the rich marine life, with species such as eels, morays, and crayfish very easy to spot on your dive. The purple and pink corals standing out in the blue sea, creating a beautiful mix of colors. The tomb of Americans, as it translates to English, is a dive that begins at 50 meters, and you need to get professional assistance to visit this dive site.
