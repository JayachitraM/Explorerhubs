Rock climbing



Best Spots for Rock Climbing in India to taste one of the best Adventure sports in the world


Rock climbing

Rock climbing is the sport or activity of climbing rock faces, especially with the aid of ropes and special equipment. The concept is to reach an endpoint, or a summit, of a rock face or structure. This can be done with specific equipment, depending on the difficulty and severity of the climb. It can be perilous, but that is often considered a positive aspect of the adventure.

Why Rock climbing in India?

India is a hub of adventure freaks as the diversity and enormous alternatives of thrilling activities favor daring minds to have enthralling experiences with their favorite sport. Gifted with incredible rock formations, including Yana Rocks, Peninsular Gneiss, Hampi Boulders, Gilbert Hill, and many more, the country provides spectacular encounters to rock climbers. If you are interested in knowing the top destinations for rock climbing in India, here are the famous sites and exquisite thrills offered.




Here we have best 5 places In India for an amazing Rock climbing experiences

  • Malshej Ghat, Maharashtra
  • Hampi,Karnataka
  • Shey Rock, Ladakh
  • Badami,Karnataka
  • Miyar Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Malshej Ghat, Maharashtra

Malshej Ghat is a quaint but popular rock climbing destination among the rock climbers of India. The vegetation is lush green and becomes all the more heavier during the monsoons. Malshej is ideal for amateur rock climbing.Snuggled in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra, it is around 120 km from both Mumbai and Pune. This rock climbing site is deemed to be suitable for beginners who want to acquire the skills of this sport. One can also explore waterfalls, caves and temples near the place. Don’t miss the breathing view from the valley, along with some beautiful flowers, birds and waterfall on the way that makes Malshej Ghat a perfect place to spend your weekend.


Duration:

3-4 days

Height:

2,297 ft

Best time to go for Rock climbing in Malshej Ghat, Maharashtra:

October and January

Level:

Moderate to Difficult

Best organizers:

Hampi – Karnataka

Hampi, the bouldering capital of India hosts one of the best rock climbing experiences. A notable UNESCO World Heritage Site, which is renowned for its ruins and fortifications. Hampi houses giant temples, palaces, forts, monolithic structures, ancient monuments and their impressive ruins.The terrains of Hampli are blessed with numerous granite boulders and you can rent a bicycle to reach the site and undertake rock climbing expeditions. A major part of the landscape is dotted with granite rocks and is a challenge for climbers which make it to the top of the list for international climbers too! Rock climbing on the quirky rock formations will give you an extricating experience.





Height:

1,481 ft

Best time to go for Rock Climbing in Hampi, Karnataka:

October – March

Level:

Moderate

Best organizers:

Shey Rock – Ladakh

Shey Rock is a monzogranite in the Ladakh region. A 20-minute drive from Leh, Shey Rock has unique formations that are suitable for both beginners and seasoned climbers. It serves to be the training ground for rock climbing by the military. There are several white rocks, and you will see a few Buddhist carvings along your route while reaching the top of cliffs. Shey Rock comprises over 40 channels to reach the top. With several routes and top-notch safety facilities, there’s no better place to climb in the Kashmir region.They have more than 20 bolted sports routes for you to reach the top to experience the breathtaking panoramic view of the mountains.



Height:

11,000 ft

Best time to go for Rock climbing in Shey Rock,Ladakh:

March – July

Level:

Moderate to difficult

Best organizers:

Thiriilophilia

Badami – Karnataka

Another site in Karnataka, famous for rock climbing on it’s raging red sandstones. Badami is the best place in India to do rock climbing on bolted routes. Badami features rock climbing over bolted routes as the steep and rugged terrains of the region host adventures that allure adventure freaks from near and far. Known to be the Mecca of Rock Climbing, the sandstone cliffs of Badami give you a chance to get involved in free climbing, rappelling, and bouldering. You can also sneak peek at the ancient temples on your way. Although climbing happens throughout the year, the best time to head to this part of Karnataka.

1,923 ft

Best time to go for Rock climbing in Badami, Karnataka:

November and March

Level:

Moderate

Miyar Valley-Himachal Pradesh

The Miyar valley is a perfect site for those seeking an adventurous trip in an isolated place. Located on the borders of Leh, this steep rock side valley and meadows offers a lot to its visitors as trekking, rock climbing, splendid views and much more. Over 50% of its terrain comprises glaciers. The valley is often referred to as ‘Himachal’s Valley of Flowers’ and has a sensational view of 15 or more snow-covered peaks. The valley is good for experienced climbers, but amateurs can also give it a try. One can witness the great Himalayan ranges while climbing the rocks. Visit the place to feel the adrenaline thrust as they climb through the extreme grounds of Miyar Valley.




Height:

13,800 ft

Best time to go for Rock climbing in Miyar Valley:

Himachal Pradesh: End of August to mid of October

Level:

Difficult
