Best Places for Canyoning for Adventure Destination


Canyoning

Canyoning is an outdoor recreational activity that includes hiking, climbing, abseiling, swimming and even cliff jumping in a natural landscape. Canyoning is all about exploring hard-to-reach natural areas by mostly following a water route down through numerous natural rock formations.


Why Do People Choose Canyoning?

canyoning a go is definitely the adrenaline rush you feel during the activity. Canyoners really get to test their own limits and feel more alive because of it. Another great thing about canyoning is that it can be enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels.



Here we have best 5 places In Newzland for an amazing Canyoning experiences

  • Leaping Burn Canyon
  • Routeburn Canyon
  • Sleeping God Canyon
  • Piha Canyon
  • Abel Tasman National Park

Leaping Burn Canyon

Breathtakingly beautiful canyon sitting on the river Matukituki, underneath the enormous mountains, Leaping Burn is among the top favorites for canyoning in south island New Zealand for adventure lovers. Plunge into crystal clear pools, abseil down the freezing waterfalls, walk along the ancient forest region and slide down the rocks to experience this canyon.



Timings:

9 AM to 3 PM approx

Best time to go for Canyoning :

15th November to 15th April

Best organizers:

Travel Triangle

Cost:

NZD$490 per adult

Routeburn Canyon

Hidden amidst the Mt. Aspiring National Park along the Lake Wakatipu shores is the mesmerizing Routeburn canyon, which will take you away from the bustle of Queenstown. Ensure an action-packed day full of abseiling, climbing, sliding, guiding, and swimming in the jaw-dropping hidden location for canyoning in Queenstown, New Zealand. It is the same place where Hollywood flicks like Lord of the Rings, and The Hobbits were shot. A 20-minute walk will lead to the starting point where you would be provided with safety information and guidance by the expert guides.


Timings:

9 AM to 5 PM approx

Best time to go for Canyoning :

October to March

Best organizers:

Travel Triangle

Cost:

NZD$319 per adult

Sleeping God Canyon

Are you willing to have a full day escapade? Then the Sleeping God Canyon is the best canyoning in New Zealand in the Coromandel region that awaits you. Rich in Maori as well as European history, explore the majestic mountains and conquer the high waterfalls in the canyon with a day packed with a thrilling experience. The descent in the canyon is vertical and has a sturdy terrain followed by sliding, abseiling, and diving deep into plunge pools.



Timings:

8:30 AM to 5:30 PM approx.

Best time to go for Canyoning :

Round the year

Best organizers:

Travel Triangle

Cost:

NZD$360 per adult

With picture-perfect landscapes and clear water of Torrent river, Abel Tasman National park lets you be amidst green bushes and blue waters. You would feel fortunate enough to have visited this exciting place to explore the raw wilderness with fearless people.
