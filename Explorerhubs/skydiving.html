Best Places for SkyDiving in India for Adventure Destination


SkyDiving


Skydiving is a term used to describe the sport of freefalling (usually from an aeroplane) through the air prior to opening a parachute. Skydiving is a great adventure sport for giving your body a sensory overload and massive adrenaline rush. skydiving, also called parachuting, use of a parachute— for either recreational or competitive purposes—to slow a diver's descent to the ground after jumping from an airplane or other high place.

Skydiving is parachuting from an airplane for fun. Skydiving can be done individually and with groups of people. Training is required. Unlike most paratroopers, skydivers often wait until they are low, before opening the parachute.

The feeling of jumping out, free fall, being under your canopy and landing your parachute is simply UNREAL. If you are afraid of the "feeling" of your skydive you are missing out on something truly spectacular. You do not get that "roller coaster" feeling that a lot of people are worried about. In order to clarify the classification of the different skydiving disciplines, we will divide them into 3 main groups: freefall, parachute flying and special jumps.

Benefits of SkyDiving


The feeling of jumping out, free fall, being under your canopy and landing your parachute is simply UNREAL. If you are afraid of the "feeling" of your skydive you are missing out on something truly spectacular.Adrenaline is known to produce improved strength and performance, as well as heightened senses. Your body also releases endorphins and serotonin during the skydive, known to make you feel happier. No matter how many times you've done it, jumping from a plane gets your adrenaline going like nothing else.It feels more like flying than falling. It's very windy, loud, and intense. Skydiving is quite breathtaking and gives you a refreshing, incredible perspective on the world.

In India skydiving is carried out from around 4,000m (or 12,500ft) offering 40 to 50 seconds of freefall time. Longer free fall times can be achieved by exiting an aircraft at altitudes much higher than 4,000m. But such jumps require pressurized oxygen within the aircraft for very high skydives.

To participate in skydiving must be physically fit, fully self-mobile with a full range of mobility and able to lift their knees to their chest unaided. Unfortunately, if you have a condition or disability which reduces your mobility, you won't be able to skydive.

Age limit for skydiving: Minimum 18 years.

Weight limit for skydiving: Minimum 40kg and Maximum 115k

Risks in SkyDiving


  • Parachute Malfunctions, Around one in 1,000 parachute openings don't go to plan, with various known malfunctions.

  • Injury on landing, if tandem students, for example, fail to lift their legs up for landing, they can take the impact through their ankles.

    Mysore – Karnataka


    Mysore is located at a distance of few hours from Banglore in Karnataka, the city has hosted many skydiving camps. Nestled at the base of Chamundi hills, Mysore is one of the best places to have the ultimate skydiving experiences in India. The skydiving camps provide the option of tandem jumps, static jumps and accelerated free falls prior to professional training for 2 to 3 days.In Tandem Jump you are with an instructor who does everything for you, accompanies you during the free fall and opening the parachute. All you have to do is to sit back and enjoy the experience. In accelerated free fall, you have to perform the task own your own, you are made to jump from a helicopter and yourself open the parachute.



    Best time to go SkyDiving Mysore:

    October to February

    Minimum age:

  • Tandem jump is 18 years.
  • Static jump is 16 years.

    • Height of the fall:

  • For tandem jump is 10,000ft.
  • For static jump is 4,000ft.

    • Timings:

    10am to 6pm

    Best organizers:

    Skyriders

    Cost:

    Rs.35,000 per person(including training sessions)

    Aamby valley- Maharashtra


    Aambey valley is located in Mulshi taluka, Lonavala near Pune in Maharashtra. The distance between Pune to Aamby valley is 90.7km. Skydive Aamby valley is an international unit with instructors and equipment from USA and Europe that will guarantee you the best vand safest skydiving packages and also an all year round membership. There will be a training sessions by experienced instructors that will guide you through the entire activity. The preparation session is 45 mins to 1hr. After that you will board a plane that will take you to the height of 10,000 ft within 30 to 40 mins. You can choose between static line jumping, accelerated free fall and tandem jump.

    Dhana – Madhya Pradesh


    Dhana is a town in Sagar district, closer to the Bhopal city, Madhya Pradesh. It is the first destination of skydiving in India. Dhana Skydiving organized event throughout the year. The training lesson for a static jump can last up to three days. The ground training is the primary training and it lasts for a day. The initial ground training is done at 4000 feet from the ground. All the professional instructors are experienced with skydiving certificates like the USPA( United States Parachuting Association). These instructors have trained students for more than a thousand jumps.
